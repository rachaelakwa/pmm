How I use AI tools

Claude as a strategic thought partner

I use Claude daily for content planning and marketing strategy. Instead of treating it as a content generator, I use it as a thought partner to stress-test positioning, map audience tensions to content themes, and uncover angles I wouldn't have considered on my own.

For Traverse's Instagram strategy, I worked with Claude to identify decision fatigue and group coordination friction as core audience pain points, then mapped those tensions to 12 distinct post concepts. Each post tied to a specific brand intent (awareness, education, engagement) and a metric signal (saves, shares, comments, profile visits).

My AI experimentation mindset

What excites me about AI in marketing isn't automation, it's acceleration. I'm curious about how AI can help marketers think more strategically, test ideas faster, and create content that's sharper and more audience-aligned. I want to explore tools beyond Claude (like Midjourney for visual inspiration, Jasper for copy variations) and bring that experimentation mindset to your consulting practice.
